# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in *haskcore*.

To maintain the architectural integrity and long-term vision of the project, I follow a *strict solo-development model*.

## Code Contributions

*I do not accept Pull Requests.* Please do not submit them, as they will be closed without review. I am fully committed
to maintaining this codebase exclusively by myself.

## How to Support

You can contribute to the project's growth in the following ways:

- *Feedback*: Open an Issue to report bugs or request features.

- *Support*: Support the development via [donation](https://numq.github.io/support).
